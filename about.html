About Us

About Haven Muse
Welcome to Haven Muse, where we specialize in creating bespoke interiors that inspire and transform. Our mission is to bring your unique vision to life through exceptional design that reflects your personality and lifestyle. With a deep-rooted passion for design excellence and a commitment to personalized service, we ensure that every project is a true collaboration between our team and our clients. Our process begins with a thorough consultation to understand your goals, followed by meticulous planning and innovative design solutions that exceed your expectations.
At Haven Muse, we offer a comprehensive suite of interior design solutions, including space planning, conceptual design, detailed design development, and project management. We also provide specialized services such as sustainable design practices, universal design principles, and post-occupancy evaluations. Our experienced team works closely with you from the initial concept to the final installation, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience. Whether you're looking to renovate your home, design a new office space, or create a stunning commercial environment, Haven Muse is here to help you transform your space into a haven of style, comfort, and inspiration.

Our Services

Space Planning & Design

At Haven Muse, we begin by understanding your lifestyle, preferences, and aspirations for your space. Our process includes conceptual design, detailed furniture layouts, lighting plans, and 3D renderings to bring your vision to life with precision and creativity.

Project Management

Our experienced project management team ensures seamless execution from procurement and vendor coordination to timeline and budget management. We prioritize efficiency and communication to deliver exceptional results on schedule and within budget.

Selection & Installation

From furniture selection to window treatments, flooring choices, and lighting design, we curate every detail to enhance your space's functionality and aesthetic appeal. Our installation and styling services ensure a cohesive and sophisticated final look.

Additional Services

In addition to our core offerings, we provide specialized services such as sustainable design practices, color consulting, universal design principles, and move management. Our commitment to excellence extends to post-occupancy evaluations to ensure your space continues to meet your needs over time.

Our Expertise

With expertise in kitchen and bath design, outdoor living spaces, and tailored solutions for specific areas like home offices and retail spaces, we bring a wealth of knowledge and creativity to every project.

At Haven Muse, we believe in creating havens that inspire joy and reflect your individuality. Whether you're renovating your home or designing a commercial space, we are dedicated to exceeding your expectations with exceptional design and impeccable service. Contact us today to begin your journey toward a beautifully designed space that truly embodies your style and aspirations.

About Our CEO

Isaacs Oluwaseun Ajibola, a creative visionary, is constantly drawn to the world of interior design. With a keen eye for beauty and a passion for transforming spaces, she continues to make her mark in the industry. Her journey is marked by a relentless pursuit of excellence, a commitment to uniqueness, and a desire to inspire others through her work.

As she navigates the world of interior design, Isaacs' talent and dedication earn her a reputation as a true artist. Her designs are a testament to her boundless creativity, and her name is synonymous with excellence. From luxurious homes to commercial spaces, her touch is sought after by those who desire a blend of style, functionality, and inspiration.

Isaacs' designs tell stories, evoke emotions, and create unforgettable experiences. Her passion project, "Spaces that Uplift," aims to transform public areas into breathtaking works of art, fostering a sense of community and joy. With every new project, Isaacs continues to push boundaries, inspiring a new generation of designers and cementing her legacy as a true icon in the world of interior design.
